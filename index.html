SAMSUNG RELEASED SOME INTERESTING PHONES IN 2020.

Samsung Galaxy

Samsung Galaxy is a line of Android smartphones manufactured by Samsung Electronics.

Samsung Galaxy Z Flip

Out of all the Samsung phones released this year, the most modern looking is the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip. This phone comes with a Snapdragon 855+ processor, 8 GB of RAM, 256 GB of storage and it has a battery capacity of 3300 MAH. The phone’s most defining feature is the fact that while it is a smartphone, it can fold in half. It also comes in many different colors, to appeal to a wide variety of tastes that consumers have.

Samsung Galaxy X Cover Pro

This Galaxy XCover Pro is a very sturdy Samsung device, and it was designed for people who work with their hands. The phone has an Exynos 9611 processor, 4 GB of RAM, 64 GB of storage and it has a battery capacity of 4050 MAH. The phone’s most defining feature is that fact that it is sturdy, and it also has a removable battery, while still maintaining IP 68 water and dust resistance. A removable battery on a phone that has these kinds of specifications is extremely rare in 2020, and this device will be sought after by those who work with their hands, and need constant connectivity, as well as those who still have an affinity for phones with removable batteries.

Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite

The Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite is a budget version of last year’s Samsung Galaxy S10. The phone comes with a Snapdragon 855 processor, 6 or 8 GB of RAM, 128 GB of storage and a battery capacity of 4500 MAH. The most defining feature of this phone is its affordability, as it is to be priced somewhere around 650$. While staying at this price point, phone provides a large 6.7 inch screen, a big battery, and better than average camera.

Samsung Galaxy S20, S20+ and S20 Ultra

The Samsung Galaxy S20 is Samsung’s flagship line and thus it holds the best specifications. The S20 comes with a Snapdragon 865 processor, 12 GB of RAM, 128 GB of storage, and a battery capacity of 4000 MAH. If you upgrade to the S20 Plus, you will receive a Snapdragon 865 processor, 12 GB of RAM, 128 or 512 GB of storage, and a battery size of 4500 MAH. Lastly, if you opt for the most expensive Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra, you will receive a Snapdragon 865 processor, 12 or 16 GB of RAM, 128 or 512 GB of storage, and a battery capacity of 5000 MAH. The primary features of the S20 line are flagship specs, sturdy design and a high-quality camera system. The S20 starts at a price tag of 1000 $, and from there the price only goes up with each model.
